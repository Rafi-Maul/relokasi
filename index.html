PT Relokasi

UD Medan Packing Service was established in 1970 by Mr. K.Shiwa Pergasen in Medan, North Sumatra. To improve the operational and management system in 2012 UD Medan Packing Service was renamed to PT. Relokasi. To provide a better relocation experience for customers in 2016 PT. Medan Packing Relocation was established as a subsidiary company of PT. Relokasi. Certified by International Association of Movers (IAM), PT. Medan Packing Relocation is committed to provide the best moving experience and ensuring customer satisfaction. Therefore, we continually strive to analyze the needs of our customers, improve our services, develop our techniques and expand our network domestically and internationally.
